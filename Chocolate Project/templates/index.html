Rating Chocolate by Country of Origin and Manufacture


This is the story of chocolate. What begins as a humble cacao seed traverses the globe, transforms into chocolate, and competes for rating from the Manhattan Chocolate Society.

The charts below are made with the data from 2015-2019. This garners 997 ratings. The cacao grown is by 54 countries different countries and the chocolate is manufactured by 56 countries.
Chart 1 (chord). This chart indicates the flow of cacao from the country that produces it to the the country that turns it into chocolate.
Chart 2 (scatterplot). This is a comparison of all 997 ratings via country. The darker the circle color, the more chocolate bars that ranked as this rating.
Chart 3 (donut). Of the 997 chocolate ratings, 483 of these are for 70% cacao chocolate bars. In order to see who produces the best chocolate, the top 10 chocolate manufacturing countries are being compared on their average ratings of the 70% cocoa bars.
